Metric ↔ Imperial Converter

Enter a value and unit to convert between metric and imperial measurements

Supported Conversions

Volume

Gallons (gal) ↔ Liters (L)

Distance

Miles (mi) ↔ Kilometers (km)

Weight

Pounds (lbs) ↔ Kilograms (kg)

Examples

  • 10L - Convert 10 liters to gallons
  • 5mi - Convert 5 miles to kilometers
  • 3.5kg - Convert 3.5 kilograms to pounds
  • 1/2gal - Convert half gallon to liters
  • 2.5/3lbs - Convert fractional pounds to kilograms

Built with Node.js and Express.js
